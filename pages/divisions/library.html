Library of the Institute

The scientifical library of the Institute functions since the day of its foundation. It services the employee and graduate student. The fund of the library has 53 942 copies (19 000 foreign publishing), formed from books, periodic publishing, author’s abstract of dissertation, methodical textbooks, bibliographic literature. There is a reading room and subscription in the library. The fund of the library keeps rarity of the publishing on microbiology, epidemiology and immunology.
